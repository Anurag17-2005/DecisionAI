================================================================================
DATA SOURCES
================================================================================

1. INTERNAL DATA (6 DynamoDB Tables)
   ├── users: Customer information, plans, locations
   ├── sales: Revenue, growth, churn by country
   ├── feature_requests: User-voted features, priorities
   ├── tasks: Company projects, assignments, status
   ├── decisions: Past decisions, confidence, outcomes
   └── competitors: Market share, pricing, ratings

2. EXTERNAL DATA (Tavily Web Search)
   ├── Quality threshold: 0.85 (high-confidence results only)
   ├── Market trends and forecasts
   ├── Industry benchmarks
   ├── Competitor strategies
   └── Best practices

================================================================================
QUESTION TYPES & AGENT BEHAVIOR
================================================================================

TYPE A: PURE INTERNAL QUESTIONS (Database Only)
────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q: "How many users are from Berlin and active?"
→ query_users(city="Berlin", active=true)
→ Returns: Count + breakdown by plan

Q: "Which country has the highest revenue?"
→ get_top_countries(metric="revenue", limit=1)
→ Returns: Country name + revenue + user count

Q: "How many decisions are approved?"
→ query_decisions(status="Approved")
→ Returns: Count + confidence breakdown


TYPE B: PURE EXTERNAL QUESTIONS (Web Search Only)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q: "What are the market trends in France?"
→ web_search("France market trends 2026")
→ Returns: Market analysis from external sources

Q: "Best practices for hiring interns?"
→ web_search("intern hiring best practices")
→ Returns: Industry recommendations


TYPE C: STRATEGIC QUESTIONS (BOTH Sources - CRITICAL!)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q: "Should we expand to France?"
→ STEP 1: query_sales(country="France") - Current performance
→ STEP 2: search_similar_decision("France") - Past decisions
→ STEP 3: web_search("France market expansion 2026") - Market intel
→ STEP 4: Synthesize recommendation with ALL data

Q: "Should we hire more engineers?"
→ STEP 1: query_tasks(department="Engineering") - Workload
→ STEP 2: get_user_stats() - User growth trend
→ STEP 3: web_search("tech hiring market 2026") - Market conditions
→ STEP 4: Data-driven hiring recommendation

Q: "Should we increase pricing by 20%?"
→ STEP 1: query_sales() - Current revenue metrics
→ STEP 2: query_competitors() - Competitor pricing
→ STEP 3: web_search("SaaS pricing trends 2026") - Industry benchmarks
→ STEP 4: Pricing strategy recommendation

Example 1: Internal Query

Example 2: Strategic Decision
──────────────────────────────
User: "Should we expand to the Netherlands market?"

Agent Process:
  1. Identifies: Strategic decision (Type C - needs BOTH data sources)
  
  2. Internal Analysis:
     - Calls: query_sales(country="Netherlands")
     - Result: €21,300/month revenue, 456 users, 24% growth
     
  3. Past Decisions:
     - Calls: search_similar_decision("Netherlands expansion")
     - Result: No similar decisions found
     
  4. External Intelligence:
     - Calls: web_search("Netherlands market expansion tech 2026")
     - Result: Market insights, competition, regulations
     
  5. Synthesized Response:
     **Internal Analysis:**
     • Current NL performance: €21,300/month (456 users)
     • Strong 24% yearly growth
     • 4th largest market by revenue
     
     **Market Intelligence:**
     • Netherlands has thriving tech ecosystem
     • High English proficiency, easy market entry
     • Competitive landscape moderate
     
     **Recommendation:**
     ✅ YES - Expand to Netherlands
     • Strong existing base to build on
     • Market conditions favorable
     • Lower entry barriers vs other EU markets
     • Suggest: Focus on Amsterdam, Rotterdam tech hubs


Example 3: Competitive Analysis
────────────────────────────────
User: "How do we compare to competitors in Germany?"

Agent Process:
  1. Internal Data:
     - query_sales(country="Germany")
     - query_competitors(country="Germany")
     
  2. External Data:
     - web_search("Germany career tech market 2026")
     
  3. Response:
     **Your Position:**
     • Revenue: €24,000/month
     • Users: 523 active
     
     **Top Competitors:**
     1. ResumeGenius AI - €19/month, 18% market share
     2. InterviewAce - €25/month, 9% market share
     
     **Market Analysis:**
     • You're competitively priced
     • Market growing at 12% annually
     • AI features are key differentiator
     
     **Recommendation:**
     • Maintain current pricing
     • Emphasize AI capabilities in marketing
     • Target enterprise segment (higher margins)
